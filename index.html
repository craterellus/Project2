A zine is a small-circulation, self-published work that's usually made up of a few pages and can include text, images, artwork, and other creative material.
This is a curated collection of zines and zine excerpts. It's meant to mimic the experience of browsing a library or used bookstore, when you open up a book to a random page and find something interesting. The types of zines featured here vary, in part to illustrate their eclectic nature, but the focus here is on intersectionality and the voices of marginalized people, along with things you wouldn't see normally in mainstream publishing. Happy browsing!
